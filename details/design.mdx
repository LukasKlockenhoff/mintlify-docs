# Introduction

Piguesso is a design system that aims to provide a consistent and cohesive user experience across our products. In this document, we will explore the key principles and components of the Piguesso design system.

## Design Principles

At the core of Piguesso's design philosophy are the following principles:

1. **Simplicity**: We strive for simplicity in both visual and interaction design, ensuring that our products are intuitive and easy to use.

2. **Consistency**: We maintain a consistent design language throughout our products, creating a unified experience for our users.

3. **Fun**: We wanted to a more colorful route in this project, to convey the focus on creativity and fun.
